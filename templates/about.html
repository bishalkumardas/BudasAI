Our Story

Why We Started BUDASAI

AI was exploding, but most content around it was either overhyped fluff or buried in technical jargon. Business owners and creators were left guessing which tools were actually worth their time and money.

We started BUDASAI to cut through the noise. We test AI tools the way real users do, on real tasks, across practical criteria, and publish findings without sponsorship influence.

Beyond ratings, we help businesses implement AI workflows that stick: automations that save hours every week, not just demos that gather dust.

"We do not rate tools to sound smart. We rate them so you can decide in 5 minutes instead of 5 days."
